Nakaseke fm

Nakaseke Radio came to air in the of 2002 under the initiative of the Uganda National Commissioner for UNESCO, Nakaseke Community Radio broadcasts on 102.9 FM.
The station operates from 06:00am in the morning and closes up between 10:00pm and 12:00am Midnight everyday.

The station's ambition is to ensure that radio and information technologies are applied to stimulate and develop sustainable capacity for rural development, reduction of poverty and minimize over-dependency for a better live-hood of the rural poors.
The radio has four (4) daily working staffs and the rest are volunteers, these come to present on the radio about different programs like Health, Agriculture, Human Rights, Education and others...
