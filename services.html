secretarial services

Nakaseke Community Telecentre provides office work services, stationery and secretarial services to all people from the community because there machines which help to ease their work.
The Telecentre now have new brand and advanced machines compared to the first ones, theses machines are very speedy and easy to use which enables to do the clients' work in a short period of Time.
We got Machines like;

  • Printer
  • Photocopiers
  • Scanners
  • Computers

People who always access these services mainly they come for Type setting paper work, Printing documents, Photocopying, scanning and others...
Civil servants, institutions, organisations, schools, individuals from the community are the major clients who access these services at the Telecentre. Schools come to do their work such as documents, typeset and printing their exams, most especially these are Junior and Primary, secondary schools and the near by surrounding schools.

conference hall

Nakaseke community Telecentre creates, organises and gives a space for any group of people who have and want to carryout their meetings.
Whoever who wants to meet people for different reasons comes to the Telecentre and seeks for the Permission such that gets authority to do so, before he/she first visit the Telecentre office and where is authorised

Local leaders and Community leaders also use Telecentre to have their meetings with their board members.
Organisations and other institutions also teach people in the community about the new strategies of overcoming the problems they have within the community, new entreprenuerships skills and others from the Telecentre.

Women Entrepreneurship Groups they get new skills from their instructors when gathered at the Telecentre as well as the farmers and agriculturalists

out reach programs

Nakaseke Community Telecentre do the community out reaches and these like are;

  • School out reaches
  • Computer out reaches
  • Civic Education out reaches
  • Internet out reaches
Out reaches are done in the community by visiting different institutions, schools, organisation and others...
All the above, each has it's own visitation day because each provides the time table at the beginning of the year.
